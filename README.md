# University of Galway Student Managed Fund

The official website for the University of Galway Student Managed Fund (SMF) — Galway's premier student-led investment society dedicated to building tomorrow's leaders in finance.

## 🌐 Website Features

### Homepage
The landing page introduces the SMF with a full-screen hero section, quick links to become an analyst or member, and an overview of the fund's portfolio.

### About
Learn about the fund's mission, history, and what sets it apart as a student-run investment organisation at the University of Galway.

### What We Do
Explore the society's core activities including equity research, portfolio management, macro analysis, and educational events.

### Team
Meet the current executive team and analysts. Each team member has a dedicated profile page with their role, bio, and background.

### Portfolio & Performance
- **Portfolio Overview** — View the fund's sector allocation via an interactive pie chart. Click any sector to see its sub-sectors.
- **Performance-To-Date** — Track individual stock returns with a bar chart and a detailed holdings table showing entry prices, current prices, weights, and contributions.

### Investment Committee
Details on the committee responsible for investment decisions and portfolio oversight.

### Events & Archive
Browse past and upcoming events, including flagship competitions like the First Year Stock Pitch and the Inaugural Stock Pitch.

### Alumni
A directory of past members who have contributed to the fund's legacy.

### Featured: Beyond The Curve
Read editions of the fund's publication, *Beyond The Curve*, covering macro trends and market insights.

### Member of the Year
Recognising outstanding contributions from fund members each year.

### Our Sponsors
Showcasing the organisations that support the SMF.

### Contact
Get in touch with the society via the contact section or linked social media channels.

### Join
Information on how to become a member or apply to be an analyst, with direct links to application forms.

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- **React 18** with **TypeScript**
- **Vite** for fast builds and hot module replacement
- **Tailwind CSS** for utility-first styling
- **shadcn/ui** component library
- **Framer Motion** for animations
- **Recharts** for data visualisation
- **React Router** for client-side routing
